1.   Introduction


Code Clan Nigeria is a platform that holds a community of mentors who reflect on their experience to individuals to grow. The mentors are categorised on domain experience and Individuals have the freedom to choose their mentors based on requirement and we make sure their needs are matched with the right expertise.

Product and Services

As a company, we believe in doing things right. We make sure people using our platform are satisfied and add great value to their careers. Our product revolves around the ideology of finding the right mentor, bridging the gap and empowering professionals to meet their mentoring needs at different stages of their career.

The service is as easy as 3 steps

  • Reach out to us and subscribe
  • Browse through our mentors and choose accordingly. Answer a couple of questions that will be put forth by us. We also offer you suggestions for a great match based on your answers.
  • We schedule a 30 min or a 45 min personalised one-on-one session with the expert

We have a dashboard in which we track & collect live data of the following:

  • User Registrations
  • User Mentor Session Requests
  • Code Clan Nigeria Chat Requests
  • Session Chats
  • Session Feedbacks From Users
  • Session Feedbacks From Mentors
  • Mentor Articles
