about us

about us

who are we?

Code Clan Nigeria holds a community of highly experienced experts who will guide ambitious individuals and programmers who are looking for a change or advance in their profession. Presently, we analyse peer activity on our various task platforms and suggest and distribute actionable insights and learning materials sourced from trending tech experts, which could make an impact and help them continuously improve.

We at Code Clan Nigeria aspire to build a trusted community of online mentors who can guide ambitious individuals and programmers to reach their goal. We aim to reach out to every individual seeking advice on different areas concerning their personal issues or professional pursuits and become Nigeria’s primary mentoring platform in this pursuit.

our values?

our mission?

Mentors in part can provide career counseling or career guidance and act as a career coach. The difference is that the role of the counselor or a coach is to share their knowledge through instruction and explanation. Mentors, on the other hand, have personal experience in climbing the job ladder and can deliver insights to career-specific challenges and opportunities. We would like to be different by not just being online career guidance or an online career counseling website but an influential, impactful and learning-oriented platform.
