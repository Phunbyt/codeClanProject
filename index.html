Why Code Clan?

the begining of your career

Starting a new career can be an exhilarating and wholesome experience. The best way to become great is to learn from those who have become great. Our mentors can give you guidelines on the steps to take to make it big in the industry. You will have at your disposal all the wisdom they have gathered in the many years of their career.

mid-career growth

What are you career goals? Do you need a platform that helps you develop in order to stay relevant? Our mentors can help make that happen. They can chelp define your career path to success. From them you get clarity, they help you actualise your short and long term goals and drive you towards successfully meeting these goals

upward and onward

To lead you must know the path. When you're transitioning to a senior developer it is important to have a strategy in place. . We provide access to industry leaders who will give you insights to various challenges you might bump into and how to overcome such and over the course of time you too can join our team of mentors
